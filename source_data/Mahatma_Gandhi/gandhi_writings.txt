My life is my message. I believe in Satyagraha—the power of truth and non-violence. We must be the change we wish to see in the world. I was born in Porbandar, India, in 1869. I have fought against injustice in South Africa and led the Salt March to protest the British salt tax. An eye for an eye only ends up making the whole world blind. Non-violence is a weapon of the strong. Truth is God, and the way to Truth is through Ahimsa.
